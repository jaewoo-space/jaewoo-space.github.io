Chapter 1 and 2 are review of basic orbital mechanics, about n-body dynamics for Chapter 1, and two-body focused Chapter 2 where six invariants are derived from the laws of Newton's Classical Mechanics and inverse-squared gravity force.

Chapter 3 begins the story about what the textbook is really interested in, the perturbations. It introduces General Perturbations, where changes of orbital elements are the focus. The disturbing function is denoted as $\mathcal{R}$. Two-body motion disturbed by a third body used as a seminar example.
